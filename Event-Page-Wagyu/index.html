2024
JAPANESE WAGYU

SHOWCASE & TASTING EVENT

Date: Monday, November 18, 2024

Venue: Let's Yori Cooking Studio,
OUE Downtown Gallery

Exclusive event designed to inspire greater use of Japanese Wagyu
in Singapore’s culinary scene. This time, we’re moving beyond premium cuts like
loins and spotlighting the often overlooked yet equally flavourful
secondary cuts such as shoulder loin, chuck short rib, and rump.

Discover innovative cooking techniques and serving styles that elevate these cuts to new heights.
(Event Commenced on December, 2024)

EVENT HIGHLIGHTS

Live Demonstration: Unlocking the Potential of Secondary Cuts

Watch a Wagyu master perform a live cutting demonstration featuring shoulder loin and chuck short rib. Witness firsthand how these cuts can be transformed and get tips on how to make the most of them in your dishes.

Wagyu Fundamentals Lecture

Gain valuable insights into Wagyu beef, its distinct cuts, and versatile applications through an informative PowerPoint presentation. This is an opportunity to understand Wagyu’s unique qualities and explore how to integrate it effectively into the Singapore market.

Cooking Demonstration by a Renowned Chef

Using the freshly cut Wagyu, a top chef will showcase inspired recipes and cooking methods, providing fresh ideas for your menu and offering new ways to enjoy these secondary cuts.
